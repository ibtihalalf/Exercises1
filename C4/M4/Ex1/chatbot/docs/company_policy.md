# Company Remote Work Policy

Employees are allowed to work remotely three days per week.

All employees must attend the Monday team meeting at 10:00 AM.

The company provides a yearly learning budget of $2000 for online courses and certifications.

Vacation requests must be submitted at least two weeks in advance.

The IT department is responsible for approving access to internal systems.

Employees should use multi-factor authentication when accessing company services remotely.

Customer data must never be shared outside the organization.

The support team works 24/7 to assist customers globally.
